Our Director

Brian Le

Team Director, head instructor

Brian Le has nearly a decade of dance training. He first discovered his passion for ballroom at Fred Astaire in Worcester, Mass. He then moved with his family to Washington, D.C. where he continued his training at Elan Dancesport. Brian then took his expertise to the next level by launching DNC2Beat in 2011. The team was active from 2011-2013 and was reestablished in 2015. During the two-year DNC2Beat hiatus, Brian spent a year with Arthur Murray training in ballroom and pursued his hunger for bachata. Through Brian's quest of technical training and thorough understanding of body movement, rhythm, and musicality, his dance style has evolved to a unique and unparalleled blend of modern bachata. His desire to share his passion for bachata has taken Brian around the country. He has choreographed alongside dancers in Los Angeles and New York and performed at events such as the Reno Latin Dance Festival and the DC Bachata Congress. He has taught workshops across the District, Maryland and Virginia, including most of the major congresses in the area.
